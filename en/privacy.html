Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Privacy is simple here: TikLive TTS does not collect your personal data. This page explains what information is processed and where.

1. The app runs locally

TikLive TTS runs on your computer. The chat messages it reads come directly from the platforms you connect (TikTok Live, Twitch, YouTube) and are processed locally to generate the voice and the overlays. The app requires no accounts and sends none of your data to project servers.

2. This website

This site is a static page: it uses no tracking cookies, no analytics and no forms that collect personal data. The typography is loaded from Google Fonts, so your browser makes a request to Google's servers when the page loads (see Google's privacy policy).

3. External links

The site links to third-party services (GitHub, Discord, YouTube, TikTok, Twitch, Kick). Each has its own privacy policy, which applies when you visit those sites.

4. Donations

Donations are made directly through the Bitcoin network or the Lightning Network. This site only displays public addresses: it processes no payments and stores no financial information.

5. Contact

For any privacy questions, open an issue on GitHub or ask in the community Discord.
